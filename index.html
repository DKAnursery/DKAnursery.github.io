Welcome to Deer Knoll Academy Nursery

Welcome to DKA Nursery, the hobby of Deer Knoll Academy HS Senior Daniel Simon. Daniel became interested in growing trees in 2020 from his cousin, and from there his hobby has grown to include trees, shrubs, wildflowers, and an assortment of vegetables. After taking a Graphic & Web Design class, he has now built a website so friends and family can easily see what plants he has available. Please enjoy browsing our website, and if you have any questions or comments, please see our Request Page.
