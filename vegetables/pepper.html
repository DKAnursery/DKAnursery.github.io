Peppers

We grew sweet peppers for the first time in 2022, and they were a smashing success! For 2023, we have expanded our selection to include last year's sweet, cayenne-shaped, Jimmy Nardello along with several bell-pepper varieties. Peppers are extremely productive; they also freeze quite well, allowing you to preserve that abundant harvest.

Jimmy Nardello

Italian Sweet Pepper

This is an Italian heirloom variety brought to the United States by Jimmy Nardello's father in the late 1800's. This variety grows in a bush habit, but may require minimal staking. Peppers are sweet and harvested when red. Said to be an excellent sweet pepper for just about everything, from fresh eating to frying to pickling. Enjoy!
