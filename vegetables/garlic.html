Garlic

Garlic is probably the most pest-free plant in any garden. This allium family bulb is related to onions and leeks. We grow garlic at our rural location because it is the only vegetable we grow not bothered by deer. Homegrown garlic is so much more flavorful than the store-bought kind, even after being stored most of the winter.
