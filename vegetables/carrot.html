Carrots

Carrots are one of the most delicious root crops commonly grown by gardeners. Rich in a number of vitamins, carrots are excellent grated into a salad or enjoyed fresh. The tricky part about root vegetables is determining when to dig them up, but carrots are great eating at nearly any stage, so this isn't as much of a problem. We are only offering one variety of carrot for the 2023 season, but we may expand our selection in the future.
