Carrots

Carrots are one of the most delicious root crops commonly grown by gardeners. Rich in a number of vitamins, carrots are excellent grated into a salad or enjoyed fresh. The tricky part about root vegetables is determining when to dig them up, but carrots are great eating at nearly any stage, so this isn't as much of a problem. We are only offering one variety of carrot for the 2023 season, but we may expand our selection in the future.

Danvers Half-Long

Fresh-Use/Storage Carrot

We are experimenting with carrots for the first time in 2023 and decided to start small. We decided to grow only one variety this year, and after much consideration (there are way more carrot varieties on the market than we would have ever imagined!), we decided on Danvers half-long. This variety is a somewhat standard, orange, 8-inch carrot. It is reputed to grow well even in clay soils, which are usually the curse of carrot growers. This variety has stood the test of time, having been developed in the late nineteenth century in its namesake town, Danvers Massachusetts. We can't wait to get started growing our first carrots ever!
