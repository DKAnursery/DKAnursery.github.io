Lettuce

Lettuce is just one of the cool-season vegetables we are growing this year at DKA Nursery. We especially like to use lettuce in salads, and we are trying out a head-lettuce and two leaf-lettuce varieties this year. Lettuces come in many different flavors and colors, so we are sure there is a variety to meet your culinary needs.

Black-Seeded Simpson

Leaf Lettuce

This large green leaf lettuce is an heirloom grown for over 100 years. Simpson lacks some of the bitter taste found in red lettuces. This variety is said to do exceptionally well in early spring planting, and ours here at DKA have germinated fast and grown quickly. We are looking forward to some fresh salad!

Igloo

Head Lettuce

This variety is a head lettuce that we are growing for its heat resistance. Another sweet green lettuce that lacks the bitter qualities of many other varieties, Burpee claims it performed well in spring, summer, and fall plantings. That almost sounds too good to be true, so we'll find out for ourselves.
