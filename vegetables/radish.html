Radish

Radishes are perhaps the most quickly maturing of any vegetable we know of. Some varieties are ready for harvest in less than 30 days for sowing! There are also longer-maturing types, called winter radishes. This year, we are sticking with the quick-growing spring radishes. These are great for interplanting, and add a nice zest to salads.
