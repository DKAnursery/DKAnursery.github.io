Welcome to DKA Nursery, the hobby and 'unofficial company' run by Deer Knoll Academy graduate, Daniel Simon. Daniel became interested in growing trees in 2020 from his cousin, and from there his hobby has grown to include shrubs, wildflowers, and an assortment of vegetables as well. After taking a Graphic & Web Design class in high school, he has now built a website so friends and family can easily see what plants he has available. Although his university studies have put gardening on hold for the near future, Daniel still has some small trees available if you are interested. He can always be reached by email, so please enjoy browsing our website, and if you have any questions or comments, please see our Request Page. Thank you!!
