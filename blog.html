Availability

This page is our effort to help you see what transplants and/or produce we have available; in fact, that is basically the whole purpose of this website! Although we do our best to keep this website updated, the best way to know for sure if a variety is currently available is to contact us. Please see the request page for more information on how we can be reached.

Below you will find a list of varieties that you can request transplants of from us. During the growing season, if we have an abundant harvest, we may also post varieties of which we have more produce than we can use. If you want to make sure you don't miss any changes, please send us an email and we will make sure you are notified of any updates! Below are the tomato, pepper, and cucumber varieties that we will grow transplants of at your request. There is also a list of bean varieties for which a limited amount of seed is available. Again, if you have any questions at all, we are more than happy to answer them. Please just check out our request page for all our contact information.

Thank you, and may God bless your garden!

The following varities are available as transplants on request:
